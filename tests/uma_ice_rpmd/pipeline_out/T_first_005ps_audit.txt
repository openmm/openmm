Conclusion
----------
Molecular wrapping is active (log line: "wrap: molecular (water O-H preserving)").
Per-atom wrap is no longer used for this driver. Despite that, centroid T_K for UMA
still diverges from TIP4P within the first few femtoseconds and far exceeds 300 K
throughout the first 0.05 ps. So something remains wrong relative to the stated
criterion — likely beyond PBC wrapping alone (e.g. ML force/thermostat balance,
centroid vs ring-polymer mode energy partition, or timestep/stiffness).
